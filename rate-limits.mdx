---
title: Rate Limits
description: Request limits by account tier.
---

To ensure fair usage across the beta, we enforce RPM (Requests Per Minute) limits based on your account tier.

| Account Tier | Requests Per Minute (RPM) |
|--------------|---------------------------|
| Beta Access | 60 RPM |
| Pro Tier | 250 RPM |
| Enterprise | Custom |

<Note>
  When rate limited, the response includes a `Retry-After` header indicating how many seconds to wait before retrying.
</Note>

If you receive a `429 Too Many Requests` response, wait for the specified duration and retry.
