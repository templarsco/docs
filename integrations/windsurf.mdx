[Windsurf](https://codeium.com/windsurf) (by Codeium) is an AI-powered IDE. Connect it to Lightweight to access all models through a single key.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Windsurf Settings">
    Launch Windsurf and navigate to **Settings** (Cmd/Ctrl + ,).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to AI Configuration">
    In the settings panel, find the **AI Provider** or **Model Configuration** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select OpenAI-compatible provider">
    Choose **OpenAI Compatible** as the provider type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter connection details">
    Configure the following:

    - **Base URL:** `https://api.lightweight.one/v1`
    - **API Key:** `lw_sk_your-key-here`
    - **Model:** `claude-sonnet-4.5` (or any model from the [catalog](/models))
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and start coding">
    Save your settings. Windsurf will now route AI requests through Lightweight. You can switch models at any time by changing the model field.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If Windsurf doesn't show Lightweight models in its dropdown, manually type the model ID. See the [Models](/models) page for all available options.
</Note>
