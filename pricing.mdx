Lightweight charges per token with **zero markup** on top of the underlying provider's prices. Your token quota covers all models — no separate billing per provider.

<Note>
  All prices are per **1 million tokens**. Your Lightweight plan includes a token quota that can be used across any combination of models.
</Note>

## How Billing Works

- **No markup**: Token prices match the upstream provider exactly.
- **Unified quota**: Your plan's token quota is shared across all models and providers.
- **Pay-as-you-go within quota**: Tokens are deducted from your quota based on the model's per-token cost.
- **Usage tracking**: Check your remaining quota anytime via `GET /v1/usage` or the [Dashboard](https://lightweight.one/dashboard).

<Tip>
  Need more tokens? Upgrade your plan on the [Dashboard](https://lightweight.one/dashboard) or contact us for Enterprise pricing.
</Tip>
